# Physical Body Part Transformation Guide

## Overview

When defeated in combat or failing encounters, enemies can transform your character by giving them physical body parts from their species. These transformations are permanent, visible, and provide stat bonuses that stack with your base stats.

---

## Body Part Types

### Primary Slots (Single Part)
- **Ears** - Replaces your human ears
- **Tail** - Grows from your spine/tailbone
- **Wings** - Emerges from your back
- **Horns/Halo** - Appears on/above your head
- **Eyes** - Changes your eye appearance
- **Teeth/Fangs** - Modifies your mouth
- **Claws** - Extends from fingertips
- **Skin** - Changes skin texture/color

### Multiple Slots (Can stack)
- **Scales** - Patches can cover different areas
- **Fur** - Can grow in multiple places
- **Limbs** - Additional appendages
- **Organs** - Internal changes

## Transformation Mechanics

### How You Get Body Parts

1. **Combat Defeat** - Random body part from winning enemy's species
2. **Failed Encounter** - Probability based on difficulty and enemy species
3. **Quest Failure** - Some failed quests give body parts
4. **Voluntary** (Future) - Arcane Lab transformations

### Probability

Body part transformation chance on defeat:
- Base: Difficulty Ã— 10%
- Species Multiplier:
  - Demons: 1.5x
  - Shapeshifters: 1.6x
  - Fae: 1.4x
  - Vampires: 1.3x
  - Undead: 1.2x
  - Others: 1.0x

Example: Losing to a Difficulty 7 Succubus = 7 Ã— 10% Ã— 1.5 = 105% (capped at 100%)

### Replacement vs Addition

- **Single Slots** (ears, tail, wings, etc.): New part replaces old one
- **Multiple Slots** (scales, fur): New parts add to existing ones

### Stat Stacking

All body parts stack their bonuses:
```
Human Base: Brawn 5, Intrigue 5
+ Cat Ears: +2 Intrigue, +1 Seduction
+ Cat Tail: +1 Intrigue, +1 Survival
+ Demon Horns: +2 Arcane, +1 Seduction, +1 Intrigue
= Total: Brawn 5, Intrigue 9, Arcane 2, Survival 1, Seduction 2
```
