# Transformation and Defeat System Guide

## Overview

The Dark Fantasy Fort Management RPG features a comprehensive transformation system where characters can be permanently altered through combat defeats, failed encounters, and magical events. This guide explains how transformations work and their consequences.

---

## Transformation Mechanics

### How Transformations Occur

1. **Combat Defeat** - When you lose in combat, enemies may transform you
2. **Failed Encounters** - Losing social, magical, or other encounters
3. **Quest Failures** - Some quest failures can trigger transformations
4. **Voluntary Transformation** - Using Arcane Labs (future feature)

### Transformation Probability

The chance of being transformed on defeat depends on:
- **Enemy Species**: Some species are more likely to transform (Demons: 150%, Shapeshifters: 160%, Fae: 140%)
- **Encounter Difficulty**: Higher difficulty = higher transformation risk (10% per difficulty level)
- **Your Corruption**: Higher corruption may increase transformation chance
- **Enemy Subspecies**: Certain subspecies have unique transformations

### Example Transformation Chances

| Enemy Type | Difficulty 5 | Difficulty 8 |
|-----------|--------------|--------------|
| Human | 50% | 80% |
| Demon (Succubus) | 75% | 120% (capped at 100%) |
| Shapeshifter | 80% | 128% (capped at 100%) |
| Fae | 70% | 112% (capped at 100%) |

## Encounter System

### Encounter Types

1. **Combat Encounter**
   - Required Stat: Brawn
   - Win: Defeat enemy, gain loot
   - Lose: Transform, lose gold/morale

2. **Social Encounter**
   - Required Stat: Seduction
   - Win: Charm enemy, build relationship
   - Lose: Seduced instead, major transformation risk

3. **Magical Encounter**
   - Required Stat: Arcane
   - Win: Overpower with magic
   - Lose: Cursed, transformed

4. **Trap Encounter**
   - Required Stat: Survival
   - Win: Avoid trap
   - Lose: Caught, transformed

5. **Ambush Encounter**
   - Required Stat: Intrigue
   - Win: Turn tables
   - Lose: Captured, transformed

### Success and Failure

**Success Results:**
- Gain gold (25-75)
- Gain reputation (+3-8)
- Improve relationship with NPC (+5 to +15)
- 10% chance to transform the NPC instead

**Failure Results:**
- Lose gold (10-40)
- Lose reputation (-1 to -4)
- Lose morale (-5 to -15)
- Gain corruption (+5 to +20)
- Worsen relationship with NPC (-5 to -15)
- **Transformation risk based on difficulty**

## Consequences of Transformation

### Stat Changes

Transformations permanently modify your stats:
- Can be **positive** or **negative**
- Stack with your base stats and species modifiers
- Multiple transformations stack

**Example:**
```
Base Character: Dark Elf (Brawn: 6, Seduction: 7)
+ Succubus Touch: +3 Seduction, +1 Intrigue
= New Stats: Brawn: 6, Seduction: 10, Intrigue: 8
```

### Corruption Gain

Every transformation and defeat increases corruption:
- Defeat: +5 to +20 corruption
- Transformation: Additional corruption based on type
- High corruption unlocks darker transformations
- May trigger special events

### Personality Changes

Defeats affect your personality traits:
- **Combat Loss**: +5 Submissive
- **Social Loss**: +10 Submissive
- **Magical Loss**: +10 Corruption

### Appearance Changes

Transformations change how your character looks:
- Visible in character sheet
- Affects NPC reactions
- Some transformations are more obvious than others

## Combat Defeat System

### What Happens When You Lose Combat

1. **Immediate Effects:**
   - Combat ends
   - Enemies may capture your NPCs
   - All characters gain corruption

2. **Transformation Roll:**
   - System checks transformation chance
   - If triggered, random transformation applied
   - Based on enemy species/subspecies

3. **Personality Changes:**
   - Submissive trait increases
   - Loyalty may decrease
   - Corruption increases

4. **NPC Capture:**
   - 50% chance non-player characters are captured
   - Captured NPCs may be ransomed or lost
   - Can affect future encounters

### Defeat Consequences by Enemy Type

| Enemy | Transformation | Capture Chance | Corruption |
|-------|---------------|----------------|------------|
| Demon | High (75%+) | 30% | High (+15-20) |
| Vampire | Medium (50%) | 40% | Medium (+10-15) |
| Shapeshifter | High (80%+) | 20% | Low (+5-10) |
| Fae | High (70%+) | 10% | Medium (+10-15) |
| Undead | Medium (50%) | 50% | High (+15-20) |

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can transformations be permanent?**
A: Yes, transformations are permanent unless reversed through special means.

**Q: Is there a limit to transformations?**
A: No hard limit, but each adds corruption and changes your character.

**Q: Can I transform NPCs?**
A: Yes! 10% chance on victory, or through Arcane Lab (planned).

**Q: Do transformations affect quests?**
A: They change your stats, which affects quest success chances.

**Q: Can I become completely inhuman?**
A: Yes! Multiple transformations can drastically alter your character.

**Q: What if I don't want to be transformed?**
A: Avoid losing encounters, keep stats high, use NPCs for risky tasks.
