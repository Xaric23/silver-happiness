## Key Features Demonstrated

1. **Clear Objectives**: Players always know what to do next
2. **Visual Progress**: Progress bars show completion status
3. **Meaningful Choices**: Decisions affect corruption, reputation, resources
4. **Chapter Structure**: Clear narrative progression through acts
5. **Multiple Endings**: Different choices lead to different conclusions
6. **Integrated Gameplay**: All story elements use existing game mechanics

## Benefits for Players

- **Guided Experience**: Perfect for new players learning the game
- **Narrative Context**: Gives meaning to your actions
- **Replay Value**: Try different choices to see all outcomes
- **Structured Goals**: Always have clear objectives to work toward
- **Character Development**: Your choices shape your character's story

## Benefits for Game Design

- **Tutorial Function**: Introduces mechanics gradually
- **Content Showcase**: Players experience all major features
- **Engagement**: Story keeps players motivated
- **Retention**: Clear goals prevent aimless wandering
- **Community**: Players can discuss choices and endings
