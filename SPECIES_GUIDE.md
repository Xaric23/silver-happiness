# Species and Subspecies Guide

This guide provides detailed information about all available species and subspecies in the Dark Fantasy Fort Management RPG.

## Species Overview

The game features 10 major species, each with multiple subspecies that offer unique stat bonuses and roleplay opportunities.

### Stat Abbreviations
- **B** = Brawn (Physical combat)
- **I** = Intrigue (Social manipulation, espionage)
- **A** = Arcane (Magic knowledge and power)
- **Su** = Survival (Resourcefulness, endurance)
- **Se** = Seduction (Erotic encounters)

---

## 1. HUMANS

Versatile and adaptable, humans are the most common species in the realm.

### Pure-Blood (B:+0, I:+1, A:+0, Su:+1, Se:+0)
- Traditional humans with no magical influence
- Balanced and reliable
- Good for diplomatic and survival roles

### Corrupted (B:+1, I:+1, A:+2, Su:+0, Se:+1)
- Touched by dark magic
- Enhanced arcane potential
- Ideal for mages and sorcerers

### Enhanced (B:+2, I:+0, A:+1, Su:+1, Se:+0)
- Physically augmented through magic or alchemy
- Strong combatants
- Good all-around fighters

### Tribal (B:+1, I:+0, A:+0, Su:+3, Se:+0)
- From the wilderness borderlands
- Exceptional survivalists
- Best for resource gathering quests

### Noble (B:+0, I:+2, A:+1, Su:+0, Se:+1)
- Aristocratic bloodline
- Skilled in court intrigue
- Excellent for espionage and social missions

## 2. ELVES

### Blood-Elf (B:+1, I:+1, A:+2, Su:+0, Se:+2)

## Recommended Builds

### Best Combat Character
**Death-Knight** (Undead) - B:+4, Su:+4, total durability

### Best Mage
**Lich** (Undead) - A:+5 arcane bonus

### Best Spy
**Shadow-Demon** (Demon) or **Changeling** (Fae) - I:+4 intrigue

### Best Seducer
**Succubus/Incubus** (Demon) - Se:+4 seduction bonus

### Best All-Rounder
**Noble-Vampire** (Vampire) or **Fallen-Angel** (Angel) - balanced high stats

### Best Survivor
**Revenant** (Undead) or **Death-Knight** (Undead) - Su:+4 survival

### Most Balanced
**Nephilim** (Angel) - +2 to everything

## Strategy Tips

1. **Match subspecies to role**: Choose subspecies based on your intended playstyle
2. **Complement with transformations**: Use transformations to shore up weak stats
3. **Consider roleplay**: Some subspecies offer unique narrative opportunities
4. **Min-max carefully**: Extreme builds (like Lich) have tradeoffs
5. **Diversify your faction**: Recruit NPCs of different subspecies for quest variety

---

## Transformation Synergies

Some subspecies work particularly well with specific transformations:

- **High-Elves** + Arcane Awakening = Ultimate mages
- **Were-Wolves** + Berserker Rage = Unstoppable warriors  
- **Succubi** + Succubus Kiss = Maximum seduction
- **Doppelgangers** + Any transformation = Perfect infiltrators
- **Liches** + Demonic Empowerment = Corruption powerhouses
