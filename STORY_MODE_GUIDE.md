# Story Mode Guide - Dark Fantasy Fort Management RPG

## Overview

Story Mode (Campaign Mode) provides a narrative-driven experience where your choices matter. Progress through four distinct acts, each with unique objectives, story events, and meaningful decisions that shape your character and the fate of the realm.

## Getting Started

### Starting a Story Mode Game

1. Launch the game and select **"New Game (Campaign Mode)"**
2. Create your character (name, gender, species, subspecies)
3. Name your faction
4. Begin your journey at the Forsaken Fortress

### Story Mode vs Sandbox Mode

**Story Mode Features:**
- Structured narrative with clear progression
- Story objectives that guide your gameplay
- Narrative events with meaningful choices
- Multiple endings based on your decisions
- Chapter-based progression system
- Character-driven story arcs

**Sandbox Mode Features:**
- Open-ended gameplay
- No structured objectives
- Complete freedom to experiment
- Focus on mechanics and optimization

## Campaign Structure

### Prologue: The Forsaken Fortress

**Setting:** You arrive at an ancient, crumbling fortress filled with dark magic and forgotten secrets.

**Objectives:**
- Survive your first 5 days
- Clear The Slums district of rubble
- Build your first room

**Key Events:**
- Arrival at the fortress
- The First Night (encounter with a mysterious figure)
- Discovery of a hidden chamber

**Duration:** Days 1-5

**Tips:**
- Focus on clearing rubble early
- Build a Recreation Wing or Brothel first
- Make careful choices in events - they have lasting consequences

### Act I: Rise from Shadows

**Setting:** Word of your presence spreads. You must establish your power and deal with emerging rivals.

**Objectives:**
- Build 3 different room types
- Reach 60 reputation
- Recruit 8 NPCs
- Accumulate 1000 gold

**Key Events:**
- Lord Malachar's ultimatum
- The Merchant Guild's proposition
- The Sorceress's gift

**Duration:** Days 6-25

**Tips:**
- Balance economic growth with military strength
- Recruit diverse NPCs with complementary skills
- Your response to Lord Malachar sets the tone for the rest of the campaign

### Act II: Dark Ambitions

**Setting:** War looms. Lord Malachar grows impatient, and darker forces emerge.

**Objectives:**
- Clear all three districts
- Reach 80 reputation
- Complete 5 arcane/transformation encounters

**Key Events:**
- The War Begins
- Betrayal in the ranks
- The Ancient Seal

**Duration:** Days 26-45

**Tips:**
- Prepare for combat encounters
- Choose your alliances carefully
- High corruption opens unique options but may lock you into darker paths

### Act III: The Final Confrontation

**Setting:** The endgame. Face Lord Malachar in a climactic showdown.

**Objectives:**
- Achieve 100 reputation
- Defeat Lord Malachar

**Key Events:**
- Malachar's final offer
- The Final Battle

**Duration:** Days 46+

**Tips:**
- Maximize your faction's power before the final confrontation
- Your previous choices influence available options
- Multiple endings are possible based on your final decisions

## Story Mechanics

### Objectives System

Story objectives appear in the "View Story Progress" menu. Each objective:
- Has a clear description of what's required
- Shows progress percentage
- Automatically updates as you play
- Must be completed to advance to the next chapter

**Objective Types:**
- **Reach Day:** Survive until a specific day
- **Build Room:** Construct rooms in your fortress
- **Reach Reputation:** Achieve a reputation threshold
- **Recruit NPCs:** Maintain a certain number of recruited NPCs
- **Accumulate Gold:** Reach a gold amount
- **Clear District:** Remove all rubble from districts
- **Defeat Enemy:** Win specific combat encounters

### Story Events

Story events trigger at specific points in the campaign. They feature:
- Rich narrative text
- Character interactions
- Meaningful choices with consequences
- Lasting effects on your faction and story

**Event Consequences:**
- Reputation changes
- Gold gains/losses
- Corruption increases
- Stat bonuses
- Morale effects
- Relationship changes
- Story branching

### Choice and Consequence

Every major story event presents choices that matter:

**Immediate Consequences:**
- Resource gains or losses
- Stat changes
- Faction status modifications

**Long-Term Effects:**
- Story paths unlock or close
- Character relationships evolve
- Ending possibilities change

**Moral Alignment:**
- Honorable choices: Higher reputation, lower corruption
- Pragmatic choices: Balanced approach
- Dark choices: Higher corruption, more power

## Multiple Endings

Your choices throughout the campaign determine your ending:

### The Dark Alliance
- Form an alliance with Lord Malachar
- Rule together as equals
- Requires: Accept Malachar's final offer

### The Noble Conqueror
- Defeat Malachar honorably
- Establish just rule
- Requires: Fight with honor in final battle

### The Dark Lord Ascendant
- Embrace total dark power
- Rule through fear
- Requires: High corruption, use all dark powers

### The Redeemed Ruler
- Show mercy and wisdom
- Break the cycle of violence
- Requires: Offer mercy at the final moment

### The Fallen Fortress
- Lose to Lord Malachar
- See your ambitions crumble
- Result: Defeat in major battles

## Strategy Guide

### Early Game (Prologue - Early Act I)

**Focus Areas:**
1. Clear The Slums quickly
2. Build economic foundations (Brothel, Treasury)
3. Recruit NPCs with diverse stats
4. Make careful story choices

**Common Mistakes:**
- Overspending on unnecessary rooms
- Ignoring story objectives
- Making hasty event choices
- Poor NPC recruitment

### Mid Game (Act I - Act II)

**Focus Areas:**
1. Expand to all districts
2. Build military strength (Armory, Scout Tower)
3. Increase reputation steadily
4. Prepare for war

**Key Decisions:**
- How to respond to rivals
- Alliance choices
- Transformation acceptance
- Resource allocation

### Late Game (Act II - Act III)

**Focus Areas:**
1. Maximize all stats and resources
2. Complete powerful transformations
3. Build elite NPC roster
4. Strategic combat preparation

**Final Preparations:**
- Achieve 100 reputation
- Build powerful room combinations
- Stock resources for final battles
- Review your choices and their consequences

## Tips and Tricks

### Optimization
- Complete objectives naturally through good gameplay
- Don't rush - take time to build your faction properly
- Balance story progress with economic development

### Story Experience
- Read all story text carefully
- Consider the long-term implications of choices
- Replay with different choices to see all content
- Take notes on major decisions for future playthroughs

### Combat Preparation
- Story mode includes mandatory combat encounters
- Ensure your player character has high stats
- Recruit strong NPCs as backup
- Use transformation bonuses strategically

### Resource Management
- Gold is crucial - maintain steady income
- Reputation opens more story options
- High corruption unlocks dark magic paths
- Balance morale to prevent revolts

## Replaying Story Mode

**Reasons to Replay:**
- Experience different story branches
- See all endings

- Make opposite choices
- Optimize your strategy

## Save System

Story progress is saved automatically with your game:
- Current chapter
- Completed objectives
- Story choices made
- Triggered events
- Progress percentages

**Best Practices:**
- Save before major story events
- Keep multiple save files
- Use descriptive save names (e.g., "Act2_BeforeWar")

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I switch from Story Mode to Sandbox?**
A: No, but you can continue playing after completing the story in a "post-game" state.

**Q: What happens if I fail an objective?**
A: Most objectives can't be "failed" - you just continue working toward them. Some story events may have failure states with consequences.

**Q: Do I need to complete all objectives to advance?**
A: Yes, all objectives in a chapter must be completed to progress to the next act.

**Q: Can I see all endings in one playthrough?**
A: No, your choices lock you into specific story paths. Multiple playthroughs are needed to see everything.

**Q: Does story mode have different difficulty?**
A: No, but story objectives provide structure that can make the game easier for new players.

**Q: What if I miss a story event?**
A: Story events trigger automatically at specific points. You can't miss them.

**Q: How long does a complete playthrough take?**
A: Approximately 50-70 in-game days, which translates to 2-4 hours of real time depending on your play style.

**Q: Is there romance in story mode?**
A: Story mode includes character interactions with NPCs, including seduction and relationship mechanics from the base game.

Whether you seek power, redemption, or something in between, your story awaits at the Forsaken Fortress.
